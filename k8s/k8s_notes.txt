4. How to Demonstrate "High Requests" in Class

Since your FastAPI apps are very small, they won't hit 50% CPU just by clicking the button once. To show the autoscaling in action, you can "stress" the pods.

    Watch the HPA:
    kubectl get hpa -w

    Generate Load:
    Run a simple loop in your terminal to hit the endpoint repeatedly:
    Bash

    while true; do curl -s http://<MINIKUBE_IP>:32000 > /dev/null; done

    The Result:
    After about 1–2 minutes, students will see the REPLICAS column in the HPA status jump from 2 to 3.
